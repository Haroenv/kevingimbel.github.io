Twitter Ad Hijacking

This page is used to test the impact of "Hijacking" other peoples sponsored tweets by replying to sponsored tweets. The idea came when I looked through the replys to a sponsored Tweet and found that a lot of people promote their own things (Books, Blogs, VLogs) by replying to paid, sponsored tweets.

From replys on Twitter this page is referenced with a Campaign Flag to track visitors. After one months of running the case study I'll publish the results.
