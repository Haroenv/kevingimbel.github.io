About

Born in the early 1990's I was raised by my mother in a small town near to Frankfurt am Main in Germany. But who want's to hear boring stories?

My interest in webdesign started in 2008/2009 when I ran my first blog and started taking pictures. So I needed a place to showcase them and created a blog (closed), a few free-hosted websites (deleted) and finally bought my own domains in 2010 (kevingimbel.de and atarijunge.de). The time has passed and I bought a new one: kevingimbel.com to be more international - because in fact the internet and all the good communities require you to be international.

Anyway after graduating from school I decided to work at a Advertisment and Design Agency called WERBEZWERG for one year. Afterwards I signed up to learn IT related things at Werner-Heisenberg-Schule in 2011, worked for 4 weeks in France in summer 2012 and graduated in 2013. I'm now a nationally certified IT Assistant.
